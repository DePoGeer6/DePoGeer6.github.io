Full Stack* Development

Frontend, backend, client-side, server-side, useless methods that get commented out—I’ve written it all. I have experience with Numpy, Matplotlib, Tensorflow, PyautoGUI, p5.js, Processing, Xcode, OpenCV, Node.js, Socket.io, Express, LaTex, and more, and I hope to expand into PHP and SQL as well. Islands.io

A unique cross between a base defense, resource collection game, and a classic, fast gameplay .io game. Play this fun game for 2-4 players with your friends in private rooms that let you enjoy all the benefits of multiplayer gaming for free in a completely online, download free environment. Islands.io has been in development for the last 6 months, and has just now entered the beta testing phase.

Parker Blended

This pandemic has been hard for everyone, so I tried to do my part by staying home, watching Netflix...and learning iOS development and becoming a licensed Apple Developer. With the pandemic came the logistical challenges of back-to-school scheduling, so I built the Parker Blended app for Francis Parker High School to help students and faculty manage their daily COVID time schedules.

Minesweeper AI

If you are like me, you’ve spent too many hours playing the Google Chrome minigames trying to beat a high score. So I made Stephen, the Google Minesweeper AI to play for me. Using the PyAutoGUI Python pip library, the program takes control of your mouse and keyboard and self navigates to the game and plays by itself. Built to work on Raspbian with the Raspberry Pi.
